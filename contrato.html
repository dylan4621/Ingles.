EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

This employment agreement (“Agreement”) is made and effective as of July 24, 2025, by and between:

Employer: Banco Financiero Nacional S.A., with a mailing address of 6ª Avenida 10-12, Zona 1, Ciudad de Guatemala, Guatemala.

Employee: Lic. Juan Carlos Rodríguez Gómez, with a mailing address of 4ª Calle 8-45, Zona 11, Mixco, Guatemala.

  1. EMPLOYEE DUTIES
    The Employee will act in accordance with this Agreement and with the best interests of the Employer in mind and agrees to adhere to any and all policies, procedures, rules, regulations, as administered by the Employer. The Employee agrees to abide by all local, county, State, and Federal laws while employed.
  2. RESPONSIBILITIES
    The Employee shall be given the job title of Senior Accountant, which shall involve: preparing financial statements, managing accounting records, ensuring compliance with financial regulations, assisting in audits, and supporting financial decision-making processes of the bank. The Employer may also assign duties to the Employee from time to time. The Employee shall be expected to work full-time.
  3. EMPLOYMENT PERIOD
    The Employer agrees to hire the Employee at-Will which means this Agreement may be terminated at any time by either the Employee or Employer.
  4. PAY
    Payment shall be distributed on a monthly basis.
  5. TRIAL PERIOD
    The Employee will not be eligible for Benefits, Vacation Time, or Personal Leave until after the first 90 days of employment.
  6. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
    During the period of employment, the Employee shall be eligible to participate in benefits established by the Employer. These include health insurance, life insurance, employee retirement plan, and professional development allowance.
  7. VACATION TIME
    After the Trial Period is complete, the Employee is entitled to 15 days off per year, which is required to be mutually benefiting for the Employer and the Employee. The Employee must provide notice before scheduling vacation in accordance with Company policy.
  8. PERSONAL LEAVE
    After the Trial Period, the Employee shall be eligible for 10 days of paid time off per year for personal and/or medical issues.
  9. FEDERAL HOLIDAYS
    The Employee shall be entitled to 10 federal holidays per calendar year.
  10. CONFIDENTIALITY
    The Employee understands and agrees to keep confidential any information that could be considered proprietary to the Employer (“Confidential Information”). Disclosure of Confidential Information, either directly or indirectly, shall result in litigation with the Employer seeking equitable relief to the fullest extent of the law, including but not limited to filing claims for losses and/or damages.
  11. NON-COMPETE
    During the term of employment, the Employee understands that he or she will be subject to learning proprietary information which could be applied to competitors of the Employer. To protect the fiduciary interests of the Employer, the Employee agrees to withhold from working in the banking and financial services industries and from working for other national or international banks operating in Guatemala. This Non-Compete shall be in effect for 1 year following the date of Employee’s termination.
  12. COMPLIANCE
    The Employee agrees to all sections of this Agreement in addition to any rules, regulations, or conduct standards of the Employer, including obeying all local and federal laws. If the Employee does not adhere to this Agreement, including any task or obligation related to the responsibilities of their Position, the Employer may terminate this Agreement without severance.
  13. AMENDMENTS
    This Agreement may be modified or amended under the condition that any such amendment is attached and authorized by all parties.
  14. SEVERABILITY
    This Agreement shall remain in effect in the event a section or provision is unenforceable or invalid.
  15. GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed under the laws in the State of Guatemala.
